Stay Protected

Campus Safety Guidelines

Your health and safety are our top priorities. Review our visual guide below for essential campus wellness tips.

Hospital Emergency Codes

Learn the color-coded alerts to respond effectively during campus medical or fire emergencies.

Heart Wellness

Prevent heart disease through daily exercise and a balanced diet rich in fruits and vegetables.

Traffic Awareness

Stay alert on the road. Drivers should avoid distractions, and students must use designated crosswalks.

Healthy Holiday Eating

Manage your sugar and salt intake during the holidays by choosing nutrient-dense whole foods.

Campus Hygiene

Maintain safety by washing hands regularly and following proper respiratory etiquette in classrooms.

Diabetes Prevention

Monitor blood glucose levels and recognize early symptoms like fatigue or blurred vision.

Infection Control

Stay updated on public health advisories and follow local guidelines for screening and management.

Back-to-School Safety

Walk with a buddy and identify trusted adults on campus to ensure a safe learning environment.
